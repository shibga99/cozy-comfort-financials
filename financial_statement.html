Strategic relevance. Cozy Comfort's annualized material spend approaches $425k with 95% concentration in a single supplier (ProSource Supply, Chatsworth CA). The current single-source posture represents a strategic vulnerability and an opening for a direct manufacturer relationship.

Margin commentary. Above-industry materials cost (38.3% vs. 22–28%) reflects retail distributor pricing through ProSource and represents the single largest optimization opportunity in the cost structure. A direct manufacturer relationship at typical OEM-to-contractor pricing would structurally compress materials toward 28–30% of revenue, expanding gross margin by ~800 basis points and lifting net margin into the 20%+ range without any revenue growth.
